About this site

I'm Virginia — imaging scientist, ultramarathon runner, working parent. I race in the UK and write about it here. These reports are honest: the fuelling that didn't work, the negative thoughts in the final miles, the people who helped, and the times I nearly called it.

There are plenty of race reports written from a place of relentless positivity and personal-best obsession. This isn't that. It's about finishing, and everything that happens between the start line and the end.

I'm also a researcher. You can find my professional work over at my CV site.
